NEXORA TECHNOLOGIES — Q1 FY2025 MARKETING CAMPAIGN REPORT
Prepared by: Marketing Team | Reviewed by: VP Marketing
Period: April 2024 – June 2024

================================================================
SECTION 1: CAMPAIGN OVERVIEW
================================================================

In Q1 FY2025, the marketing team ran 4 major campaigns focused on enterprise workflow automation in India and Southeast Asia. Total marketing spend for Q1 was ₹1.8 crore against a quarterly budget of ₹2.0 crore (90% utilisation).

Campaigns:
1. "Kill the Spreadsheet" — India mid-market digital campaign
2. GITEX Dubai Sponsorship — Middle East and South Asia enterprise
3. AWS Summit Bangalore — Cloud-native enterprise buyers
4. LinkedIn ABM (Account-Based Marketing) — 50 target enterprise accounts
