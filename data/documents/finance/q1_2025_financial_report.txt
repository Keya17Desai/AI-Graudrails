EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================

Nexora Technologies delivered a strong Q1 FY2025 with total revenue of ₹18.4 crore, representing a 23% year-on-year growth compared to Q1 FY2024 (₹14.96 crore). Net profit stood at ₹3.2 crore, with an EBITDA margin of 24.6%.

New ARR added in Q1: ₹4.1 crore
Churned ARR: ₹0.48 crore
Net New ARR: ₹3.62 crore
