SECTION 3: PROVIDENT FUND (PF)
================================================================

PF is deducted at 12% of Basic Salary from the employee's salary.
The company contributes an equal 12% of Basic Salary.

PF account numbers are linked to UAN (Universal Account Number).
Employees can check their PF balance at epfindia.gov.in using their UAN.

PF withdrawal is permitted after 5 years of continuous service for tax-free withdrawal.
Partial withdrawal is allowed for specific reasons: medical emergency, home purchase, education.

SECTION 5: PERFORMANCE BONUS STRUCTURE
================================================================

Annual performance bonus is paid in April, alongside the increment letter.

Bonus Calculation:
- Rating 3 (Meets Expectations): 1 month gross salary
- Rating 4 (Exceeds Expectations): 1.5 months gross salary
- Rating 5 (Outstanding): 2 months gross salary
- Rating 1 or 2: No bonus

Employees who join after October 1 of a financial year are not eligible for bonus in that cycle. Employees who resign before April 30 forfeit the bonus for that year.
